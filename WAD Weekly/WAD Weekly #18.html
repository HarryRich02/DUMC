🧗 LAST WEEK IN THE DUMC

DUMC vs DUHWS Social
This annual competition took place on Wednesday with a very positive outcome for the superior society. Shenanigans occurred, including cross-dressing, running, and desperately trying to convince any of the hill-walkers to drink.
    Thanks to a strong showing from our resident alcoholics, I am happy to announce that the DUMC has wrested control of the trophy back from mountain leaders!
NULSCC
After a long and stiff journey to Warwick, the gumbies stepped out onto the tarmac to be greeted with the beautiful sight of lead walls, another cheerleading competition, and lots of swimmers. Briefings were held and Redbulls were consumed in record time in a last ditch effort to get our endurance up.
    Climbs were attempted in a slightly random starting order throughout the session, with alumni Minipher topping both looking as cool as a cucumber to make finals, Alasdair and Rachel sailing up both routes, myself tweaking out on a questionable clip, and Joe getting rightfully rinsed for taking 30 seconds to clip at the top of the M1 route, in which the commentators took great joy in. They also took great note of the bedazzling Durham team kit - I’m sure Ethan would have been honoured in his absence, draping us in the finest of Castore robes.
    Post-climb scran was devoured (£12 for a premade “woodfired” pizza?) and we watched on as the speed ‘climber’ crew soared up the walls (really just another Team GB spawnpoint). Finals commenced quickly after this, treating us to both speed and lead where Minipher gave an amazing battle on the wall to come 4th on a decently horrible route, making good progress up to the third headwall. Overall, Durham did fantastic (especially considering the lack of lead training done) and we have some results to be very proud of!
- Miranda
Eden Rock Student Comp
Some people too scared of ropes for NULSCC took part in the Eden Rock Student Comp on Saturday as well. I'm told lots of sending occurred, including impressive results from the baby fresh, for many of whom this was their first comp!
    The ever-impressive Toby came first despite an injured pulley, with Fran coming in a close second. This was all topped off by some local beer and a huge pizza.
