Sendfest
The single most important competition in the DUMC calendar was borne upon us on Saturday in the form of Sendfest, which saw many of our members venturing to the DCC for a crack at 25 interesting competition climbs, split over two sessions. Encouragement was awarded in the forms of shared beta and t-shirts which clearly went down a treat, decking out DUMC in their numbers! Max and Miranda made finals in 1st, retaining their positions with 3t 3z and 2t 3z respectively to win both categories, with Mila 3rd, Rachel 4th and Maddy 6th to show a strong representation of DUMC women in finals 💪
    Overall, a very memorable, fun competition as always, with an amazing turnout of lots of familiar faces, good work from everyone who took part!
    P.S. please send in photographs for social media!
- Miranda
